Consulting Culture
01

The 5-5-5 Rule

Every week, reach out to 5 new people, follow up with 5 existing contacts, and have 5 meaningful conversations. Consistency beats intensity.

02

Lead with Value

Before asking for help, share a relevant article, make an introduction, or offer a unique insight. The best networkers give before they receive.

03

The Warm Intro Chain

Don't cold-email partners directly. Map your 2nd-degree connections and ask for warm introductions through mutual contacts.

04

Build in Public

Share your learning journey on LinkedIn/Twitter. Post market analyses, book takeaways, and industry observations. VCs and consultants notice active thinkers.

05

The Informational Interview Formula

Ask: "What's one thing you wish you knew before entering VC/consulting?" → Listen deeply → Follow up with a thank-you note + relevant article within 24 hours.

06

Target the Middle

Don't just network with senior partners. Associates and principals are more accessible and often have more influence in hiring decisions.

07

Community Before Application

Join VC/consulting communities (On Deck, Reforge, Product School alumni). Being part of the ecosystem signals commitment.

08

The Deal Flow Hustle

For VC specifically: Source and send interesting deal flow to VCs. Nothing proves your eye for startups better than actually finding good ones.
